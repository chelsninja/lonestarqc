Mission Statement

Lone Star Quidditch Club, or LSQC, is an adult athletic organization based in Texas that provides the opportunity to play the sport of quidditch. Our mission is to develop players into high-caliber athletes and compete for championships on regional and national scales. LSQC offers a competitive atmosphere for veteran and first-time players alike, while emphasizing elite skill development as well as community growth. While LSQC is dedicated to excellence on the pitch, the program also strives to provide a space for fostering friendships, leadership, and sportsmanship.
