Mission Statement

Lone Star Quidditch Club is an adult athletic organization that provides the opportunity to play the sport of quidditch within the state of Texas. Our mission is to develop players into high caliber athletes and compete for championships on a regional and national scale. LSQC offers a competitive atmosphere for veteran and first-time players alike, emphasizing elite skill development as well as community growth. While LSQC is dedicated to excellence on the pitch, the program strives to provide a space for fostering friendships, leadership, and sportsmanship.
